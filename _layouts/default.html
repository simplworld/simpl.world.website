Use the tech you already know & love.

Simpl is a simulation platform built with Python, Django, React.js, Crossbar, & Autobahn. Simpl gives you powerful tools for creating fun and effective educational experiences with your data.

100% Free & Open Source

Simpl is a completely free open source project, license coming soon.

Flexible & Customizable

The ability to customize the platform to your exact needs and for various simulations.

Fast & Effective

Data updates in real time; results and changes can be seen immediately.

Utilize Simpl's Core Platform Components

SIMPL-Games-API

Stores the data and provides relationships between data.

SIMPL-Modelservice

Provides the business logic, math and other behaviors.

SIMPL-React Library

Provides the UI and other front-end components.
